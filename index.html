Welcome to the most exciting reality show you've never heard of!

The Challenge is a reality competition show that puts existing reality stars against each other in several weeks of physical, mental and endurance competition. The show has been airing on MTV since 1998 and in that time, has completed 36 seasons (Season 37 is currently airing as of November, 2021). The show's audience and production has grown over its 20+ years. The Challenge has given out $1 million too the winner each of the last five seasons and now features contestants and locations from around the world
