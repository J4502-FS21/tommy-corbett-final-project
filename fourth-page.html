Johnny Bananas

Johnny Bananas is by far the most iconic Challenger of all time. He's competed in 20 Challenges from 2006-2020. Over that time he's accumulated 62 Daily Challenge wins, competed in 9 Fina Challenges and won 7 of those, making him the winningest Challenger ever.

7 wins

Total earnings: $1,184,720

Ashley Mitchell

Ashley Mitchell came onto The Challenge in 2013 from 'Real World: Ex-Poison'. She's competed in 9 Challenges and has 2 wins. Ashley is most famous for keeping a $1 million prize all to herself instead of splitting it with her partner and ex-boyfriend in one of the most intense moments of the entire show.

2 wins

Total earnings: $1,121,250

Chris "CT" Tamburello

Since CT began competing in The Challenge in 2004 he has been a fan favorite. He first competed in season 8: The Inferno and has since competed in 18 seasons (including the current season: 37). In recent seasons, CT has been the bennifactor of felow competitor's fear to go against him in elimination rounds and finals. This intimidation comes from years of experience and a few instances of physical altercations that cut some of his seasons short.

4 wins

Total earnings: $965,000
